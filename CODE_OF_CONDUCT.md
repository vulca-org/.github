# Vulca Systems Community Standards

We expect respectful, evidence-oriented collaboration. Discuss the work rather
than attacking people; distinguish observed facts from inference; preserve
privacy and attribution; and make room for cultural, disciplinary, and
technical differences.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate exposure of private material,
and manipulation of evidence are not acceptable. Maintainers may edit, hide,
lock, reject, or remove contributions that violate these standards and may
restrict participation to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns through a previously verified private maintainer route.
Do not expose sensitive conduct reports in public issues.
